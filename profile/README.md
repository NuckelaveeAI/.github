# Nucklavee

**Nucklavee** is an infrastructure composed of a set of MCP servers, an agent, required side services, and integrations that together form my small digital slave.

## Philosophy

When creating **Nucklavee**, I am guided by one single desire: to build a thing that will handle all the routine work — the _bad shit_ — that is somehow connected to the Internet. As a side effect, the agent also gets the ability to use its powers not for their direct purpose, but to create _good shit_. For example, it may post a photo of an apricot to my Telegram channel at 3 AM, or print my will if my pulse goes above a certain threshold. This is very funny, so the value of **Nucklavee** immediately doubles.

In short, my offspring has two goals:

1. Reduce the amount of _bad shit_ in my life.
2. Increase the amount of _good shit_ in my life.
